Safety information

  • The information is provided by users, it might be inaccurate or wrong.
  • Always obey the CERN safety rules, specially the Radiation Protection safety rules.
  • Respect any signals and traffic signs that you encounter. Respect the velocity limits in the road and in any case, always circulate below the speed of light.
  • Use your common sense. If you go to a clearly marked dangerous area without protection because "you read on a website that it was OK", you only have yourself to blame.
  • Don't be an asshole. Make sure you do not disturb people while they are doing science.
